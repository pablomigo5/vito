Our Story

Founded in 2015 with a passion for craftsmanship, Vito Designers has grown from a small local shop in Kwekwe to a trusted name in custom tailoring and home decor. We believe every stitch matters and are dedicated to delivering excellence.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, custom-made products that bring joy and comfort to our clients. We source the best fabrics and pay meticulous attention to detail to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Vito Designers. By ordering any of our products, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions.

1. Orders & Payments

All orders are made via WhatsApp and are confirmed upon our reply. A deposit may be required for custom orders. Final payment details will be communicated during the confirmation process.

2. Turnaround Time

Estimated completion times will be provided when you place your order. While we strive to meet these deadlines, please note that these are estimates and not guarantees.

3. Alterations & Returns

For custom-made clothing, we offer one complimentary alteration to ensure a perfect fit. As items are custom-made to your specifications, we do not accept returns or offer refunds unless the product is faulty.
